Choose commands

Start typing keywords or describe what you'd like to do to see a list of matching commands already defined in the system. If none match what you want, click [Add new command] and enter the new command you want the developers to implement.

    Build your scenario

    Your chosen commands appear here - drag-and-drop items to reorder, and click the scenario name and description to give your scenario meaningful values. Once you're happy with your scenario, click the "Select scenario" button (or manually select the scenario text with your mouse) and cut and paste it into your feature tracker or testing code.
